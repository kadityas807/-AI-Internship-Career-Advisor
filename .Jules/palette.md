## 2025-05-15 - [Onboarding UX]
**Learning:** Multi-step processes (like onboarding) should include a visual progress indicator (step counter and progress bar) and support keyboard navigation (e.g., 'Enter' to proceed). In multi-line inputs ('textarea'), avoid mapping the 'Enter' key to form submission or navigation if it prevents users from creating new lines.
**Action:** Always include progress markers and keyboard shortcuts in multi-step forms, ensuring 'Enter' is context-aware for textareas.
