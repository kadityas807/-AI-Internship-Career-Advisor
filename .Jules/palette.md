## 2025-05-15 - [Onboarding Accessibility & UX]
**Learning:** Adding a visual progress indicator and semantic labels significantly improves onboarding clarity and accessibility. However, be cautious with keyboard shortcuts in multi-line inputs (`textarea`), as mapping "Enter" to "Next" can interfere with expected text entry behavior.
**Action:** Always provide visual progress in multi-step flows. Ensure `label` elements are properly associated with inputs. Avoid "Enter" as a submission trigger in textareas unless specifically requested.
